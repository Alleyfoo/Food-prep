## Known issues / limitations
- 22 pairings resolve to corpus 0 (olive_oil, pasta_water, grilled_fish as a
  single entity, smoked_yogurt, etc.) — honest "no corpus evidence", not a bug.
  The override map `CANONICAL_TO_CORPUS` can be extended.
- Meal-repair phrase extraction is regex-based ("I have X and Y"); unusual
  phrasings ("my plate is X with Y") may not trigger. `_plate_phrases` covers
  "i have / i've got / i got".
- Potato `salad` technique is reachable only via component-first / branch list,
  not "I salad'd the potatoes" (intentional — that phrasing is unnatural and
  `salad` collides with tomato raw_assemble).
- Scout pairings still hand-curated, not corpus-rarity-derived (future work).
