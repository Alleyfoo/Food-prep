### Important constraint (do not violate)
The model used in this environment **does not support image reading** (no
vision). Any workflow step that tries to have the model visually inspect a
PNG must be **skipped** — it will crash/hang. Verify screenshot file
integrity with PIL (`Image.open(p).verify()`) instead of asking the model
to look at them. This is why the screenshot commit explicitly notes "no
visual inspection."

  to read them (see constraint above).
