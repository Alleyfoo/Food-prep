## Known issues / limitations
- `repairs` / `avoid_when` are surfaced only via `filler_profile`; not yet used
  by `plate_balance` (deliberate). A future, well-considered integration could
  let the engine prefer fillers whose `repairs` match the plate's condition
  (heavy/dry/acidic) and caution on `avoid_when` matches — but the
  plate-condition vocabulary must be pinned down first to avoid flattening.
- `_fillers_for_role` returns top-N by confidence; medium-confidence new
  fillers (sauerkraut, fresh_herbs) may be outranked by classic high-confidence
  fillers on a given plate. They are in the Cook pool (verified by
  `test_new_fillers_are_cook_suggestable`) but not always in the top-4 shown.
- `_detect_subject` is substring-based (like `_detect_ingredient`); very short
  filler names could in principle over-match, but longest-wins and the
  full-ingredient branch gate keep the common prompts correct.
- 22 pairings still resolve to corpus 0 (olive_oil, pasta_water, …) —
  `CANONICAL_TO_CORPUS` can be extended.
